Recommended start

Pick a home country with a large home base (USA, UK, or Canada give you the best Anglo-adjacent pool). A franchise-heavy opening into 2–3 low-distance markets maximises scale with low risk. Save at least $1.5M for Year 2 growth.

  • Enter 2 franchise markets in Year 1 (never 3+ — there is an entry penalty).
  • Source globally in Year 1 (2% margin), switch to local when the Trade War hits.
  • Avoid WOS in BR/CN/EG/IN/ZA — Political Crisis in Year 5 forces those out.
